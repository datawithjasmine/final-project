Our Story

Humble Beginnings Studio Four Visuals, born from the visionary mind of urban photography enthusiast Jasmine Mitchell, has become a distinctive force in capturing the raw beauty of city life through its lens. Established in 2023, this Chicago-based photography studio has carved its niche by specializing in urban aesthetics, blending modernity with a timeless flair to create evocative images that resonate with the pulse of the city. Purpose The team at Studio Four Visuals shares Jasmine's passion for urban aesthetics. Comprising talented photographers and creatives, the team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of traditional photography to create images that tell a story beyond the surface. Their collective vision transforms the studio into a hub of innovation, where modern techniques meet a deep appreciation for the timelessness of urban beauty. As Studio Four Visuals continues to evolve, its dedication to urban aesthetics remains unwavering. The studio actively collaborates with local businesses, architects, and urban planners, fostering a symbiotic relationship that allows for a deeper exploration of the city's visual identity. With an ever-growing portfolio that mirrors the dynamic nature of urban life, Studio Four Visuals stands as a testament to the power of photography in revealing the captivating stories embedded within the concrete jungles we call home.
